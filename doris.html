Mapo tofu is a classic Sichuan dish featuring soft tofu cubes cooked in a spicy, umami-rich sauce made with ground pork, doubanjiang (chili bean paste), and Sichuan peppercorns for a signature numbing effect.

Xiaolongbao, or soup dumplings, are delicate steamed dumplings filled with seasoned pork and a rich, flavorful broth, created by incorporating gelatinized stock into the filling before steaming.

Peking duck is a famous Chinese dish known for its crispy, golden-brown skin and tender meat, traditionally prepared by air-drying the duck, glazing it, and roasting it to perfection.
